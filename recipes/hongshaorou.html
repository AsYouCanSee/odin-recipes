红烧肉

Description

This recipe will describe how to prepare 红烧肉 (hong2shao1rou4), a classic Chinese brasied pork dish perfect for going along with a bowl of freshly steamed white rice.

I suggest pairing this dish with a side of vegetables to cut down on the fat which could make the meal heavy to

Ingredients

  1. 500g of pork belly (skin or skinless)
  2. 2 tsp of oil
